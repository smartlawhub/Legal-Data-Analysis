Counsel in the case   The claimant was represented by the law firms NautaDutilh, and Bentsi-Enchill, Letsa &amp; Ankomah. During the merits phase the law office of Mitchell Madden also joined the claimant’s team. Ghana was represented by Dewey and LeBoeuf LLP, Reindorf Chambers in Ghana, and Dutch lawyer Jacomijn J. van Haersolte-van Hof. At the merits phase, and following the collapse of Dewey and LeBoeuf, the respondent was advised by DLA Piper and Reindorf Chambers.
