Venezuela refused to pay the advance on costs but is awarded all costs nonetheless   The decision of the tribunal meant that Venezuela’s abuse and forum-shopping arguments did not need to be reviewed.  The unsuccessful claimant was ordered to pay Venezuela’s legal fees, and the cost of the arbitration, which Blue Bank had already entirely advanced, given Venezuela’s failure to make an advance payment of its share.
