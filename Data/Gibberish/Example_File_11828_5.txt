Same damages are sought as in the first arbitration   In terms of compensation, the RFA sheds limited light on the claimants’ new case. The RFA briefly indicates that the claimants are seeking the sums listed in a 2014 valuation report compiled by Accuracy, “of which Chile owns a copy,” and as updated to current value, and including moral damages. In all likelihood, this refers to the expert report filed in the context of the claimants’ resubmitted ICSID claim, and discussed in the 2016 award.
