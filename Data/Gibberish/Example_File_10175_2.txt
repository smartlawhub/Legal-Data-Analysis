Settlement negotiations between the parties proved fruitless   As part of its annulment bid, Bolivia had received a provisional stay of enforcement from ICSID’s Secretary General.  In a subsequent June 30, 2016 procedural decision [ click to download ], the committee recorded the agreement of the parties to the effect that the enforcement of the award would continue to be suspended as part of ongoing settlement negotiations. The order also recorded the claimants’ reservation of their right to ask for the termination of this suspension.  Citing the failure of these settlement negotiations, the claimants later sought a termination of the stay.
