Investor’s resubmission not an abuse of process   Mexico’s also argued that the resubmission, seen in the light of the first case and the domestic proceedings, should be viewed as an abuse of process.  The tribunal however was satisfied that the claimant “properly submitted [its claim] within the framework of the remedies open to it” – which was not deemed abusive.  Since this was a clear-cut situation, the tribunal refrained from offering a broader opinion as to its “inherent power” to refuse jurisdiction in face of abuse. Thus, it would fall to future tribunals to explore this question further.
