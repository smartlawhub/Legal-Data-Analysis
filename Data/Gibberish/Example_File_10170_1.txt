Arbitrators agree to hear one of Mexico’s jurisdictional objections on a preliminary basis, but decline to shift seat of NAFTA proceedings     Last December , we reported on Mexico’s failed bid to have its NAFTA dispute with Canadian investor Lion Mexico Consolidated LP dismissed on an expedited basis.  Since then, the tribunal of Juan Fernandez-Armesto, David J.A. Cairns and Ricardo Ramirez Hernandez has released a new decision on May 29, 2017 [ click to download ] partly granting Mexico’s request to bifurcate the case to decide the jurisdictional objection at the heart of the earlier expedited proceedings.  The claimant is assisted by Mayer Brown, Mexico by government counsel.
